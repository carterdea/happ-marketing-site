Rules

Happ relies on the community to share and post the incredible things happening around us. By updating the map with valuable content, we can help each other explore and engage in the beautiful world around us. Below is a short list of our guidelines:

  • No nudity or anything sexually suggestive
  • Don't post anything illegal
  • No threats, bullying, or harassment
  • Don't give out any personal information (yours or other people's)

Breaking any of these rules will result in a lifetime ban. If you find any posts or people chatting you who break these rules, please report them in the app so we can ban them.
